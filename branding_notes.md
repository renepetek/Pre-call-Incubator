# User Branding Reference - KST Marketing

## Key Visual Elements

**Primary Colors:**
- Gold/Yellow: Used for headlines, accents, dividers, and call-to-action buttons
- Black: Bold condensed typography for main headlines
- Red: Small accent squares/markers (similar to reference page)
- White: Clean background

**Typography:**
- Bold condensed sans-serif for main headlines (e.g., "How to Build an Online Tutoring Business with AI")
- Yellow subheading text for emphasis
- Black body copy

**Layout & Structure:**
- Centered hero section with bold headline
- Yellow subheading with key value proposition
- Large bordered video frame (dashed gold border) with play button
- Yellow call-to-action button with white text and dashed border
- Case study cards with gold names, black body text, video thumbnails
- "Why should you trust us?" section with testimonials
- Revenue badges in gold (e.g., "From 0 to $11K/mo in 30 days")

**Key Differences from Reference:**
- Gold/yellow accent instead of red
- More prominent use of dashed borders on video frames and buttons
- Gold typography for names and key metrics
- Similar direct-response structure but warmer color palette

**Branding Tone:**
- Educational, results-focused
- Emphasis on student success stories and revenue outcomes
- Clear progression from value prop → hero video → social proof → case studies
